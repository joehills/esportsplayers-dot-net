E.S.P.O.R.T.S.

Electronic ShakesPeare Or Random Theatre Shows

Why is this electronic?

Half a decade ago, members of the Vanderbilt University Theatre Program attempted to encourage folks to read ShakesPeare plays aloud for fun.

Inspired by their efforts, our community attempts to carry on their tradition, adapting their techniques for the digital age.

Be the E.S.P.O.R.T.S. player you want to be!
